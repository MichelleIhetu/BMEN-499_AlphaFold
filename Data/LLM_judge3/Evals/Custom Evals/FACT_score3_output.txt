INTERPRETATION
----------------------------------------------------------------------
  F (Fidelity) is expected to be highest in extractive fallback
  mode since answers directly contain KB signal phrases.

  A (Accuracy) depends on whether numeric values from the KB
  appear in the answer. Questions that retrieve KB-001 or KB-014
  score highest on A since those passages are statistics-dense.

  C (Completeness) rewards answers that cover disorder evidence,
  mechanism, quantification, limitation, and recommendation.
  Extractive answers covering multiple KB passages score higher.

  T (Traceability) scores how many distinct KB passages contributed
  facts to the answer. Answers drawing from 3+ KB entries score
  highest since the RAG system retrieves top-3 passages per query.

  Low FACT scores indicate questions where the retrieved passages
  were topically mismatched, reducing all four sub-scores together.
