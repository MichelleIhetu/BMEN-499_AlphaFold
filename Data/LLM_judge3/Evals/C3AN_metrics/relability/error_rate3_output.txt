INTERPRETATION
----------------------------------------------------------------------
  E6 (Redundancy) is expected to be the most frequent error in
  extractive fallback mode because the same KB passages are
  retrieved repeatedly across questions, causing identical phrases
  to appear in multiple answers and sometimes within one answer.

  E4 (Retrieval-Question Mismatch) flags cases where BiomedBERT
  retrieves semantically similar but question-mismatched passages.
  This is the core weakness of dense retrieval without re-ranking.

  E3 (pLDDT Inversion) should be near zero since the KB passages
  correctly state pLDDT directionality. Any flags here indicate
  a retrieval combination that creates an implicit contradiction.

  E5 (Numeric Hallucination) should also be low since answers
  are extracted directly from the knowledge base built from the
  actual DisProt dataset statistics.

  Loading BioMistral-7B would primarily reduce E4 (by generating
  question-specific answers) and E6 (by synthesizing rather than
  concatenating passages), at the cost of potentially increasing
  E5 and E8 if the model generates rather than extracts facts.
