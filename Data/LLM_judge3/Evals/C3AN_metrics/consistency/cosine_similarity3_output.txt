INTERPRETATION
----------------------------------------------------------------------
  Q-A similarity measures how well the answer addresses the question.
  A-P similarity measures how grounded the answer is in the retrieved
  passages (high = extractive, low = hallucinated or drifted).
  Q-P similarity measures retrieval quality -- whether BiomedBERT
  retrieved passages relevant to the question.

  In extractive fallback mode (BioMistral not loaded), A-P should
  be very high since answers ARE the passages. Lower Q-A scores
  indicate the retrieved passages did not directly address the
  specific question asked.
