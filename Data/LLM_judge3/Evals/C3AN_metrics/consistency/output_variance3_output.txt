  INTERPRETATION:
  LLM Judge 3 uses extractive fallback, which concatenates the
  same top-3 KB passages repeatedly. This leads to low answer-
  length variance (answers are structurally similar) but high
  topic drift (retrieved passages may not match the question).
  Low V4 entropy confirms KB-009/KB-011 dominate retrieval,
  reducing output diversity. Loading BioMistral-7B as the
  generator would increase V2 TTR and reduce V8 topic drift
  by synthesizing passages into question-specific answers.
