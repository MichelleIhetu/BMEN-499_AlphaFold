INTERPRETATION
----------------------------------------------------------------------
  LLM Judge 3 (BioMistral RAG) uses an extractive fallback when
  BioMistral-7B is not loaded. The extractive mode concatenates
  retrieved passages directly, which can produce internal
  contradictions when the top-3 retrieved documents assert
  conflicting claims (e.g., 'pLDDT is the most reliable signal'
  AND 'experimental data takes precedence').

  C7 (Experimental vs computational precedence) is expected to be
  the most frequent contradiction because KB-009 ('pLDDT is the
  most reliable signal') and KB-011 ('experimental data takes
  precedence') are frequently retrieved together.

  C4/C5 (Proline/Glycine signal strength) contradictions arise
  because KB-004 labels proline a 'strong predictor' while KB-006
  qualifies glycine as needing combination -- but KB-005 also
  notes glycine 'adds conformational freedom', creating tension.

  TO REDUCE CONTRADICTIONS: Run with --no-gen flag removed and
  BioMistral-7B loaded. The generative model synthesizes retrieved
  passages into a coherent answer that resolves conflicts, rather
  than concatenating them directly.
