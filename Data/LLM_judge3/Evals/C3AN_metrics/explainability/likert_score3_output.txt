INTERPRETATION
----------------------------------------------------------------------
  Strongest dimension : L4 -- Biological Specificity
  Weakest dimension   : L2  -- Causal Explanation

  LLM Judge 3 in extractive fallback mode scores well on L4
  (Biological Specificity) because KB passages are rich in domain
  terms (pLDDT, IDR, MoRF, DisProt). L3 (Uncertainty) scores
  moderately because the KB includes gray zone language. L2
  (Causal Explanation) and L6 (Actionability) are typically
  lower in extractive mode since causal synthesis requires the
  generative model (BioMistral-7B) to connect retrieved facts.
  Loading BioMistral-7B would improve L2, L5, and L6 scores.
