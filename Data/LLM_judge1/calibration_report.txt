WHAT IS CALIBRATION?
----------------------------------------------------------------------
  Calibration checks if a rule's confidence score matches
  how often it is actually correct.
  Example: A rule claiming 85% confidence should be right
  about 85% of the time. If it is only right 60% of the
  time, it is OVERCONFIDENT and needs adjustment.
