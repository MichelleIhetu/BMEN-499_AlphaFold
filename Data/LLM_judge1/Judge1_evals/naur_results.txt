WHAT IS THE NAUR SCORE?
----------------------------------------------------------------------
  NAUR measures how well predicted answers overlap with ground
  truth using three chunk sizes:

  Unigram  (n=1, weight=50%) -- single word matches
    Captures key term and vocabulary coverage.

  Bigram   (n=2, weight=30%) -- two-word phrase matches
    Captures local phrasing similarity.

  Trigram  (n=3, weight=20%) -- three-word chunk matches
    Captures semantic chunk-level similarity.

  Final NAUR F1 = 0.5 x Unigram + 0.3 x Bigram + 0.2 x Trigram

  Score interpretation:
    0.7 - 1.0 : STRONG MATCH   -- prediction closely matches truth
    0.4 - 0.7 : MODERATE MATCH -- good overlap, some gaps
    0.2 - 0.4 : WEAK MATCH     -- partial overlap
    0.0 - 0.2 : POOR MATCH     -- little overlap
