WHAT IS AGREEMENT SCORING?
----------------------------------------------------------------------
  Agreement scoring checks whether LLM1 predictions say the
  same things as the ground truth across five dimensions:

  1. SEMANTIC CONSISTENCY (weight=30%)
     Do both texts express the same core concepts?
     Measured by shared content word overlap after removing
     common stopwords like 'the', 'is', 'and'.

  2. KEYWORD AGREEMENT (weight=25%)
     Do both use the same biomedical domain terms?
     Checks for terms like: disorder, pLDDT, IDR, Pfam,
     proline, glycine, threshold, backbone, etc.

  3. NUMERIC AGREEMENT (weight=25%)
     Do the numbers match? e.g. if GT says 29.1% does the
     prediction also say approximately 29.1%? Tolerance: 2%.

  4. DIRECTIONAL AGREEMENT (weight=20%)
     Do both texts agree on the direction of claims?
     e.g. both say 'conservative' not one saying 'aggressive'.

  5. CONTRADICTION PENALTY
     Each detected contradiction reduces the score by 0.05.
     Max penalty capped at 0.30.

  Score interpretation:
    0.75 - 1.0  : STRONG AGREEMENT   -- texts express same claims
    0.50 - 0.75 : MODERATE AGREEMENT -- mostly agree, minor gaps
    0.25 - 0.50 : WEAK AGREEMENT     -- partial agreement
    0.00 - 0.25 : DISAGREEMENT       -- significant conflicts
