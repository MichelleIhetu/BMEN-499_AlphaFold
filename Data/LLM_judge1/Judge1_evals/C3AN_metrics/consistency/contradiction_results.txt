WHAT IS CONTRADICTION COUNTING?
----------------------------------------------------------------------
  Contradictions occur when the LLM prediction states something
  that directly conflicts with the ground truth. Three types:

  TYPE 1 -- NUMERIC CONTRADICTION
    Prediction states a different number than ground truth.
    Example: GT says 29.1% but prediction says 50%.
    Tolerance: numbers within 3% are NOT contradictions.

  TYPE 2 -- DIRECTIONAL CONTRADICTION
    Prediction uses an opposite directional word to GT.
    Example: GT says 'reliable' but prediction says 'unreliable'.

  TYPE 3 -- FACTUAL NEGATION
    Prediction negates a claim the ground truth makes.
    Example: GT says 'strongly correlates' but prediction
    says 'does not correlate'.

  Severity: HIGH = negations + large numeric gaps (>50%)
            MEDIUM = directional opposites + smaller numeric gaps
  Severity score = HIGH*1.0 + MEDIUM*0.5
