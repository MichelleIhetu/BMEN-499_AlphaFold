WHAT IS FACTSCORE?
----------------------------------------------------------------------
  FactScore evaluates factual precision by decomposing each answer
  into atomic facts and verifying each one against ground truth.

  Atomic fact types:
    NUMERIC     -- number or percentage claim
    COMPARATIVE -- above/below/higher/lower claim
    CAUSAL      -- causes/promotes/disrupts claim
    EXISTENCE   -- contains/has/found claim
    PROPERTY    -- is/are/called claim

  Verification:
    SUPPORTED    -- fact confirmed in GT       (full credit)
    PARTIAL      -- fact partially confirmed   (half credit)
    UNSUPPORTED  -- fact contradicts or absent (no credit)
    UNVERIFIABLE -- cannot check from GT alone (no credit)

  FactScore = (supported + 0.5 x partial) / total_facts
  Score: 0.0 (all wrong) to 1.0 (all correct)

  References:
    Min et al. (2023) FActScore: Fine-grained Atomic Evaluation
    of Factual Precision. EMNLP 2023.
    Maynez et al. (2020) On Faithfulness and Factuality in
    Abstractive Summarization. ACL 2020.
