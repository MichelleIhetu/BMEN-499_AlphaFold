HOW PREDICTIONS ARE MADE
----------------------------------------------------------------------
  Step 1 -- BiomedBERT encodes each question into a semantic vector
  Step 2 -- Each symbolic rule's topic is also encoded
  Step 3 -- Cosine similarity finds the most relevant rule
  Step 4 -- Calibrated confidence scores weight the match
  Step 5 -- The best matching rule's answer becomes the prediction
  Step 6 -- Prediction quality is flagged (HIGH / MEDIUM / LOW)
