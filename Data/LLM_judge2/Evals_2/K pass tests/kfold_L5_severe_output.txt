INTERPRETATION
----------------------------------------------------------------------
  L5 Severe is the most demanding configuration. Positive labels
  are reserved for proteins with disorder content >= 0.85 AND a
  region >= 30 aa. This means very few proteins pass -- expect
  low recall and high precision. The McNemar test checks whether
  fold variability is random or systematic. ECE < 0.05 means the
  predicted probabilities are well-matched to actual positive rates.
  This level is appropriate for high-stakes annotation tasks where
  false positives are more costly than false negatives.
