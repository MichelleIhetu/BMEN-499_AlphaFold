WHAT IS FACTSCORE?
----------------------------------------------------------------------
  Decomposes each answer into atomic facts and verifies each
  one against the ground truth.
  SUPPORTED=full credit | PARTIAL=half | UNSUPPORTED=no credit
  FactScore = (supported + 0.5 x partial) / total_facts

  For Vanilla RAG: FactScore reveals whether retrieved passages
  are factually grounded in the GT or introduce unsupported claims.

  Reference: Min et al. (2023) FActScore. EMNLP 2023.
