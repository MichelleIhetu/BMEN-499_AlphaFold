WHAT IS BERTSCORE?
----------------------------------------------------------------------
  BERTScore measures semantic similarity using contextual token
  embeddings from BiomedBERT trained on PubMed abstracts.
  Captures meaning even when different words express the same
  concept -- unlike NAUR which requires exact word matches.

  Precision = how much of pred is semantically in GT
  Recall    = how much of GT is semantically in pred
  F1        = harmonic mean of precision and recall

  Score: >=0.90 VERY HIGH | >=0.80 HIGH | >=0.70 MODERATE
         >=0.60 LOW       | < 0.60 VERY LOW
