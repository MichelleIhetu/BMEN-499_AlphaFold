WHAT IS THE GUARDRAIL SYSTEM?
----------------------------------------------------------------------
  A guardrail validates BioGPT answers against DisProt ground
  truth BEFORE they enter the evaluation pipeline.
  This reduces hallucination risk in the baseline answers.

  5 GUARDRAIL LAYERS:
  Layer 1 -- NUMERIC GUARD
    Checks BioGPT numbers against known DisProt statistics.
    Tolerance: 10%. Flags ungrounded numbers.

  Layer 2 -- HALLUCINATION GUARD
    Checks ratio of BioGPT content not found in GT.
    >60% ungrounded = HIGH risk, >40% = MEDIUM risk.

  Layer 3 -- CONTRADICTION GUARD
    Detects opposite directional words vs GT.
    e.g. GT says 'reliable' but BioGPT says 'unreliable'.

  Layer 4 -- DOMAIN TERM GUARD
    Checks for missing biomedical terms present in GT.
    Flags answers missing IDR, pLDDT, Pfam, etc.

  Layer 5 -- CONFIDENCE GUARD
    Checks answer length and question relevance.
    <20 words = HIGH risk, <40 words = MEDIUM risk.

  VERDICTS:
    PASS  -- no issues or LOW only -- use BioGPT answer as-is
    WARN  -- MEDIUM issues -- use with warning annotation
    BLOCK -- HIGH issues  -- replace with GT fallback answer
